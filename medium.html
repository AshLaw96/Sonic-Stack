How to Play:
Keyboard- Use left and right keys to move blocks side to side. Hold or click down arrow key to move block down quicker. Press space bar to turn blocks.
Buttons- Use left button and right button to move blocks side to side. Click down button to move block down quicker. Press twist button to turn block.

Make full horizontal line to gain points.
